Chrome Extension

Playlist Organizer for Spotify

Reorder songs within Spotify playlists by moving tracks to the top, middle, bottom, or an exact position.

Privacy Policy Source / Support

What it does

  • Move tracks to the top of a playlist.
  • Send tracks to the bottom or middle in one action.
  • Choose an exact target position for any track.
  • Work directly inside the Spotify web player.

How it works

After signing in with Spotify, the extension adds reorder actions to the Spotify web player so you can reorganize large playlists without dragging tracks one by one.

Permissions used

  • identity to sign in with Spotify.
  • storage to store Spotify session data locally.
  • accounts.spotify.com and api.spotify.com to authenticate and reorder playlist tracks through Spotify's API.
